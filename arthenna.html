Art henna

About the Event :

Mehandi competition is an event where participants showcase their skills in creating intricate henna designs on hands or feet. The competition focuses on factors like design complexity, creativity, symmetry, and the richness of the henna stain.

Rules :

  • Maximum 20 participants
  • Theme: Bridal Mehandi Designs
  • Mehandi cones will be provided
  • Participants must bring a person for design application
