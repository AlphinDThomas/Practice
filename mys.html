Mystique Quest

About the Event :

Are you ready to embark on a thrilling adventure across the campus? Solve riddles, complete challenges, and race against time to uncover hidden clues leading to the ultimate treasure! Gather your team, put on your thinking caps, and let the hunt begin!

Rules :

  • Clue-Based Challenge: Each clue will lead to a new location, but you must complete a task at every clue point before moving to the next.
  • Time Limit: Teams must finish the hunt within the allotted time to be eligible for winning.
