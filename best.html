Best Actor

About the Event :

BEST ACTOR, the platform where you can be the spotlight by showcasing your passion.

Rules :

  • Preliminary Round : Movie Scene Reenactment
  • Each participant must prepare a 5-minute-long movie scene in advance.
  • The audio clip of the selected scene will be played, and the participant must act it out accordingly.
  • Lip-syncing, expressions, body language, and overall performance will be judged.
  • Intermediate Round: Property Round
  • Each participant will be provided with a random prop.
  • The participant must incorporate the given prop into their performance creatively.
  • The duration of the act is 2 minutes.
