Duo Dance

About the Event :

Join us for an exciting Face Painting Competition! Ten participants will have the chance to showcase their creativity and talent. The theme for the competition will be provided on the day of the event, and all necessary materials will be supplied.

Rules :

  • The theme will be provided on the spot.
  • Participants will have five minutes for preparation before the competition begins
  • Mobile phones will be confiscated after the five-minute preparation period and will be returned after the competition.
