Duo Dance

About the Event :

The Duo Dance Competition is an exciting three-level event where pairs compete to showcase their synchronization, creativity, and technique.

Rules :

  • Each team must consist of 2 members
  • Maximum performance time: 5 minutes
  • No props will be provided by the organizers
  • 1st round - online
  • 2nd round - On stage performance with prepared choreography
  • 3rd round - Spot choreography round
  • Duo can consist of any gender combination:
    • A boy and a girl
    • Two girls
    • Two boys
  • Vulgarity or obscenity will lead to disqualification
